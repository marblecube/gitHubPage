Chinatown Excursion

A captivating journey through the heart of Los Angeles' Chinatown. This video montage captures the vibrant essence of Chinatown, beginning at the light rail station and weaving through both modern developments and the timeless beauty of Old Chinatown. Experience the charm of paper lanterns and traditional architecture, culminating in a serene ride back on the train with mesmerizing views from the window.

This piece, recorded during the earliest days of the COVID quarantine, is part of Firecracker Media's portfolio and stems from the "Red One" project, proudly supported by a grant from U.S. Artists.
