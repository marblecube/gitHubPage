As part of its development, Project: Thea operates as a research and pilot program aimed at exploring new possibilities in interactive media. Project: Guardian

A small, blonde haired, blue eyed child holding a stuffed animal.

A dramatic science fiction novella that explores the journey of a boy chosen to become a guardian in a futuristic world governed by AI and an unseen council of administrators. As he navigates his role, the story delves into themes of duty, identity, and the complex relationship between humanity and technology. Currently in the outline phase, this project is set to offer a thought-provoking and immersive experience as it progresses towards completion.
