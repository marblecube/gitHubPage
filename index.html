Current Projects

Project: The Red One

A black and white image of a small silhouette of a seated man looking over a jungle at a star-filled sky.

Project: Red One is a multimedia adaptation of Jack London's iconic short story, blending narrative, visuals, and sound design into a unique cinematic experience. Set in a remote jungle, the story explores the discovery of a mysterious artifact that changes the lives of those who encounter it. Drawing on London's themes of survival, mystery, and the untamed forces of nature, Red One brings a fresh, immersive approach to a classic tale, fusing traditional storytelling with cutting-edge media techniques. Currently in prep, this project promises to captivate with its powerful blend of adventure and the unknown.

Project: Theogeny

A close up of Zeus with lightning crackling about his head and shoulders and his eyes glowing with a energy.

Project: Theogeny features AI-generated video animations that explore the origins and evolution of the Greek pantheon, starting with the Greek Creation Myth and progressing through the key elements of Hesiod's Theogony. The project brings to life the emergence of Gaia, Uranus, Eros, and more, offering a compelling and immersive look into the myths that shaped ancient Greece and one of history’s most enduring traditions. Recently Completed Projects

Project: Storyteller

An illustration of a serene nighttime forest setting with a group of people sitting in a circle around a glowing campfire. At the center of the circle, an elderly storyteller with a long white beard gestures as he shares a tale. Above them, the word 'STORYTELLER' appears in fiery, glowing text, blending with the warm hues of the fire. The surrounding trees and starlit sky add a magical and inviting atmosphere.

Project Storyteller is a cutting-edge Python-based platform that powers interactive, relationship-building “choose your own adventure” storytelling experiences. It combines Gradio for user-friendly voice and text chat interfaces, Ollama’s locally hosted LLMs for dynamic narrative generation, and ElevenLabs’ API for lifelike narration, creating deeply engaging, personalized story interactions. Designed with portability in mind, the system’s adaptable architecture opens doors for future applications in robotics, where it could enable interactive, emotionally resonant storytelling with humanoid companions or other AI-driven devices. Project Storyteller represents the next step in immersive, choice-driven entertainment and beyond.
