About

Firecracker Media is a one-man show founded by Vidal Palacios who is mostly an actor, writer, and director and who has crew and production/development experience and also works in full-stack web development and AI research and development for media applications.

Projects

Current Projects

Here are our main current projects:

Project Pagoda

A full screenplay; a supernatural Japanese gothic horror story, is currently seeking representation.

Project Thea

An interactive storytelling experience that uses a character acted by an AI model.

Ongoing Projects

Here are our ongoing projects:

Project Pipeline

A sub-project of Project Thea involving creating the technical stack (API calls, RAG databases, and programmatic logic, etc.) necessary to bring Thea to life.

Project PersonaForge

A sub-project of Project Thea, Project Persona Forge focuses on fine-tuning Thea's personality, ensuring she exhibits unique traits and responses that engage users authentically. This project integrates advanced AI techniques to shape Thea into a compelling and lifelike interactive character.

Project StoryForge

A sub-project of Project Thea, Project StoryForge is dedicated to structuring Thea's narrative using a sophisticated RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) database. It aims to enhance storytelling by providing Thea with rich, contextually relevant information that adapts dynamically to user interactions, creating a seamless and immersive story experience.

Project Hometown

A collection of documentary photographs taken in a suburban landscape alongside these same photos reworked via artificial intelligence.

Project Community Outreach

The creation of this anchor website and associated social media prescences.
